About

Parra Macro is a macro research and geopolitical risk consultancy focused on delivering actionable intelligence for navigating global markets.

What We Do

We combine quantitative analysis with real-time data feeds to provide a systematic view of geopolitical risk and its impact on financial markets. Our tools process thousands of news sources daily through NLP-driven sentiment analysis to produce risk scores across six key indicators.

Our Approach

  • Data-Driven — We use NLP sentiment analysis, GDELT event monitoring, and multi-source news aggregation to build composite risk scores.
  • Real-Time — Market data and geopolitical indicators update continuously throughout the trading day.
  • Global Coverage — Risk scoring for 190+ countries with priority tracking of 50 key nations.
